What is Aimer?

Aimer is a website made just for fun and learning for myself. Here i have practiced my html, css and javascript learning to make something working. It is made totally for learning purpose and does not try to provide any good service to any user. But, I hope my friends will be happy seeing out what I am upto. Hope you like this website.

How does it work?

Simple for the user, but was not that simple for me to make, learnt a lot making it. Just go to the Get Started page and follow the steps being mentioned. I hope you will understand(I hope,:)).

Background:

I have used quite good amount of javascript, not as much as the industry people, but not too less for a begineer, not flexing :). the website take your input goals and runs a "bubble sort" to identify which goals you are most interested in.

Dont Worry about your input data.

Theres no back-end to the server. No database connectivity, no server calls, nothing, just front-end stuff. So dont worry about me spying on your secret goals. To be honest, I would love to know your secret goals.
